About Mike

Who Am I?

I’m Michael Jackson …., sorry I meant to say Mike Destiny and I am a brand designer by day and a fullstack web developer by night.
Been in the freelancing scene for a little to a year now, before that I worked at Cre8labs, as a fullstack web developer later on transitioned to being a brand designer, and eventually juggling between the two roles.
As a kid I have always been an artist, sketching at every chance I got and as a troubled teenager I developed an interest in psychology, I read everything I could find on psychology: my journey into brand design came from these two inclinations, art and psychology.
As a programmer (fullstack web developer) I am deliberate and meticulous; not a rockstar programmer, but a problem solver, and as a brand I stay creative and insightful.
So When you hire me, you get two minds(perspectives) working on your project considering the benefits and implication of doing certain things in a certain way.

Goals & Aspirations

Help As Many Small Businesses As I Can

Helping as many small businesses as I can; this goal is what actually propelled me into programming on android, at that time I just thought if I could get as many small businesses as I can to go mobile, get on android it would revolutionize the way small businesses do business. Especially in the area of the north where I was, technology isn’t really that advanced (forward) and the people (businesses) aren’t exactly business savvy forward minded, this here is the reason why I failed at that time and had to pivot into web technology.
